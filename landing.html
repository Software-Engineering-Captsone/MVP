How it works

Three steps to your first partnership

Whether you are an athlete or a brand, getting started takes just a few minutes.

1

Create your profile

Athletes connect their social accounts and build out their portfolio. Brands set up their company details and describe their tone.

2

Discover your match

Brands search and filter through athletes by sport, school, reach, and engagement. Athletes get surfaced to brands that fit their niche.

3

Close the deal

Send proposals, agree on terms, and manage everything from your dashboard. No back and forth across emails and DMs.
